Gaming Technology

The Evolution and Sales since the Inception.

This purpose of this project is to show some statistics about the state of technology in the gaming world. This information is shown by providing things such as news articles about recent technological upgrades and a graph detailing sales numbers for the best selling consoles per generation. The articles and graphs are also accompanied by images obtained from Flickr by using their api to get results based on various gaming-related tags. The graphs and Flickr images are put into a slider that the user can cycle through.
